Welcome!

This study explores how people make decisions in real-life contexts..
In each question, you will see two items that are characterized by two features.
You then have to decide which of these two items scores higher on a third feature that is shown in the question.
Please make a decision in each question based on your intuition. There are no correct or wrong answers.

Now you will be presented with the questions as shown in the example below, each question will offer two options; F and J.
To answer the questions, please use the keyboard buttons F and J on your keyboard.
